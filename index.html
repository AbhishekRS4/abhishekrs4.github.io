I am working as a Research Associate in Autonomy at Ati Motors. At Ati Motors, we are on a quest to build autonomous cargo vehicles.

At Ati, I've worked on various problems in the areas of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. Some of my work include 1) lateral pose estimation of camera with respect to parallel lane markings with camera at a known height from the ground, 2) segmentation of humans from lidar point cloud data using PointNet 3) drivable area estimation by segmenting ground points from point cloud data using PointNet 4) drivable area estimation by semantic segmentation using camera data etc.

Previously I worked as a Project Assistant Intern at HPC lab in SERC, IISc. Bengaluru, India, where I worked on implementation and benchmarking of Data Parallel Algorithms to achieve high performance on hardware accelerators.

I obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology from National Institute of Technology Karnataka, India in 2016.
